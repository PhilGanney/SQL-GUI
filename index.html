In this MVP version we assume you can start making tables in at least First Normal Form (1NF)

SQL GUI does not force databases into a structure or Normal Form beyond 1NF though you will find tips and reminders aimed at developers who do not frequently do Database Normalisation.

SQL GUI doesn't connect to a DBMS, you copy the SQL to where it's needed
